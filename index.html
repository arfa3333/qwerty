TeenVerse

Guidance

TeenVerse is a branch of HackClub in Pakistan. HackClub is a global non-profit network of high-school coding clubs where students learn to code by building real projects. Founded in 2014, it empowers teenagers to become makers, leaders, and problem-solvers through hands-on learning and collaboration. Students run their own clubs, lead workshops, and work on creative tech projects-ranging from websites and appd to harware and games.HackClub promotes a supportive, open-source community that encourages experiments and innovation, helping young people gain pratical coding skills while making friends and having fun.
